Speaking Topics

01

Planning Parenthood in the Age of Autism

Drawing on the research behind her bestselling book, Nyema walks audiences through the environmental, nutritional, and biological factors that shape neurodevelopment. Evidence-based, non-alarmist, and profoundly practical.

02

The Research Parents Are Not Being Given

A frank examination of the peer-reviewed literature on childhood health that rarely makes it into mainstream medical consultations. Nyema presents the evidence, explains the gaps, and helps audiences understand why informed consent matters more than ever.

03

Natural Approaches to Childhood Illness

A clinical overview of safe, evidence-supported natural interventions for common childhood health conditions, from ear infections and fever management to digestive health and respiratory illness. Suitable for parents and healthcare professionals alike.

04

Autism Research: What the Evidence Actually Shows

A rigorous, evidence-led presentation on the current state of autism research, separating myth from method and presenting the strongest clinical evidence in a form that resonates with both lay audiences and sceptical professionals.
