Author Bio for Press

Nyema Hermiston is a Registered Nurse, Naturopath, and Homeopath with over 30 years of clinical experience. She holds a Bachelor of Science with Honours from Middlesex University, London, and serves as President of The Aurum Project, a charitable organisation dedicated to advancing homeopathic research worldwide. She is the author of four published books on natural health, neurodevelopment, and evidence-based parenting, including Planning Parenthood in the Age of Autism. Her work has been described by medical professionals as "rigorous," "compassionate," and "exactly what parents and practitioners need."
