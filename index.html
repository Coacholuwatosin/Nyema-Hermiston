The Author

A Practitioner Who Writes From The Inside Of Her Field

Nyema Hermiston has spent over three decades working at the intersection of nursing, naturopathy, and homeopathy. Her writing does not come from theory. It comes from clinical rooms, research libraries, and a lifelong commitment to giving families the information mainstream medicine rarely shares.

As a Registered Nurse, Naturopath, and Homeopath with a BSc Honours from Middlesex University London, and the current President of The Aurum Project, Nyema brings a rare combination of academic rigour and practical compassion to every page she writes.

Methodology & Integrity

The Research Behind The Work

"Every claim I make is backed by the literature. Every recommendation I give is one I would give my own family."

— Nyema Hermiston

Nyema Hermiston does not write from opinion. She writes from three decades of clinical observation, peer-reviewed research, and a commitment to translating complex science into practical guidance that real families can use.

Her books draw on environmental medicine, nutritional science, developmental biology, and homeopathic research to give parents a complete and honest picture of the factors that influence their child's health from the very beginning.

This is evidence-based writing in the truest sense. Not filtered through corporate interests. Not softened for a particular agenda. Written by a practitioner who has spent a career asking the questions mainstream medicine has been slow to answer.
